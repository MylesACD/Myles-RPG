About Techniques

Purpose

Techniques are supplemental abilities unique to your character, that you chose how they grow more powerful. The limit of a techniques power is determined by its tags, which are general descriptors of what your technique does with different point costs associated. Generally techniques can be anything that fits narratively for your character and within the power limits. They are not exclusive for combat situations, to create a technique that does not do damage select the boon/bane tag†. To make techniques an important aspect of a characters they will have multiple techniques but each can only be used once per session. The GM will create custom partial success options for each technique.

Crunch

  • Characters have a pool of points equal to 10+2*LVL.
  • A technique can have a maximum value of ⅔ of a character's total pool†.
  • Players choose which attribute each technique rolls
  • There are four tiers of tags with increasing cost†.
  • Some tags have multiple tiers, which require more points.
