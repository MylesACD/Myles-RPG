Jade Hand

History needs review

Long ago during the Anno Novis a Jotschael mage, Spytchey, bound a high-lord of O'Zura into an adamantine flask. After the Uruk-hero Gilgrach killed Spytchey, she went through the three trials required to open the flask and released the dao, in return for their freedom Nahice offered Gilgrach a wish. She wished for the power to destroy Khus Thauk, the Claws of Domination, so that their demonic power would no longer taint Ethos. The strength of Gilgrach's will was so overwhelming that as Nahice forged the gauntlet that would crush one of the accursed blades, the dao was made into the jade giving it their full power and sentience.

Years later when Gilgrach's quest to find one of the Khus Thauk was finnally successful, she gripped the blade in the massive jade gauntlet, closed her fist, and turned the sword dust. But there is no power in Mortus, Jotunhiem, or Hell that can withstand Damn'niz'Khus. And as the blade was annihilated so was Gilgrach, leaving behind a crater nearly a mile wide. All that remained was the Jade Hand, now completely black, smoking at the bottom. The crater became a holy site and a large city, named Crater, grew around it.

Abilities

Deep Jade
As an action you can use the Jade Hand to absorb a magical effect within reach. Additionally as a reaction to spell being cast within your sight you can completely absorb it into the Jade Hand. The Jade Hand has a jade capacity of 65.

Children of Nahice
As an action you can spend stored magic charges to create a jade elemental.

Minor (gargoyle), 5 cost
Petty (Earth Elemental), 9 cost
Major (Dao), 13 cost


Dao's Gift
You can spend 9 charges to cast any 6th or lower level spell from the Jade Hand.


Spell Casting Focus
The Jade Hand is a spell casting focus which grants +4 to spell attack rolls, spell save DC, and spell damage.

Elemental Connection
You are fluent in True Terran and Primordial.

The Jade Hand is part of you. Either die or lose the hand permanently, not even wishes can't help.
