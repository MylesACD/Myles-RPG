Yetkilii - dragons (with authority)
Temelilii - elemental dragons (the main nine)
Madenilii - metal dragons (subset of the main nine)
Zerrailii - dust dragons (oxy, carbon, sulfur, arsenic)
Cevikilii - fey dragons very nimble

Tesir: A form of socio-arcane currency that dragons exchange amongst themselves and other phylums
tesirama bırakıyorum: I yield some of my tesir to you
Dürende: The social-hierarchy of the individual dragons. The higher on the hierarchy, the more tesir a dragon has.

Demir: adamantium, strength, fortitude
Valide: A mother Yetkilii, nessacarily being very old and powerful and having lots of demir.

Sarsaq: nonmagic mortal, stupid, foolish
üstüne ölü yakılması: A place where a wyrm was killed by an Iron dragon. Roughly means fiery tomb of the great.

Teme: Acrazel, Quultar, god
