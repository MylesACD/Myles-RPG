Puff


Mechanical Effects

All methods: Gain a swim speed of 60 ft.

Each use method includes all the benefits of the previous ones.


Live Injection: You gain advantage on acrobatics and initiative checks.


Augmented Injection: You can take the dodge action as a bonus action. Your speed increases by 10 ft.


Synthetic Inhalation: Attacks against you cannot have advantage. When you are subjected to an effect that allows you to make a Dexterity saving throw, you take half damage. Your speed increases by an additional 10 ft.
