Species

This is a comprehensive list of the different species of Ethos. They are split into four categories: mortal, extraplanar, non-sapient creatures, outworlders.

Mortals

Mortals are the most exclusive of all creature types, having a special role cosmologically as their existence was the primary motive force behind most of Quultar's creations. In general, mortals are much more intelligent than non-extraplanar creatures but rather feeble individually, although some can rise to near Celestial power. The advancement of an mortals is singly due to their widespread cooperation and organization, which allows each individual to contribute more than normally able. The most unique characteristic of mortals is that they are the only creatures created with souls, garnering the title of "children of Quultar", positioning them as the central focus of the history of Ethos. Colloqially the definition of mortal has expanded to include other sapient, finite-lived creatures such as elfs, minotaurs, and gnomes that otherwise generally fit the above description. The vast majority of mortal species resisde in Mortus being native to that sphere, but there are many sub-species of human influenced by extraplanar entities.
